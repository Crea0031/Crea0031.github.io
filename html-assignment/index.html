About Me!

My name is David "Dave" Crean and I was born in Ireland, but currently reside in Toronto, Canada. This is my 4th year in Toronto and have made some wonderful memories during the years but I hope to move to Ottawa to start a new adventure. I am passionate about technology, digital design and woodwork. I have exceptional experience in IT support, specifically focused on Apple devices. I am certified as an Apple iOS and Mac technician. I have an undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering from the Waterford Institute of Technology and I am currently pursuing an Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College.
