About Me!

My name is David "Dave" Crean and I was born in Ireland, but moved to Canada 4 years ago. I am passionate about technology, digital design and woodwork. I have exceptional experience in IT support, specifically focused on Apple devices. I am certified as an Apple iOS and Mac technician. I have an undergraduate degree in Civil Engineering from the Waterford Institute of Technology and I am currently pursuing an Interactive Media Design program at Algonquin College.

I hope to improve my photography skills and develop skills in web development and User Experience over the next 2 years. I hope to pair my theoretical engineering knowledge with practical skills from the design program to create excellent websites, apps and overall user experience.
