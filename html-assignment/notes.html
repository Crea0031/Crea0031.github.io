Here is a list of 5 HTML tags and their uses.

HTML Tag: <html></html>
This is the most important tag on a HTML website. Any details or code most be added inbetween these tags.
Heading Tag: <h1></h1>, <h2></h2> etc.
This is used to create the heading of a page and depending on the number after the h will determine the heirachy of that heading. For example h1 is the most important heading and h6 is the least important heading.
Body Tag: <body></body>
This is the main body of the document. All the content of the web page must be added inside the opening and closing tags.
Form Tag: <form></form>
This tag is used to create forms on your website to gather details such as name, date of brith or email and password etc.
Article Tag: <article></article>
This contains any independent piece of content on a page.
